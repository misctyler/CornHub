Super Auto Pets Weekly Tournaments - FAQ

Welcome to the Super Auto Pets Weekly Tournaments! We're excited to bring you a fun and casual way to play Super Auto Pets and compete for $CORN with other players. Here's everything you need to know to participate:

What is Super Auto Pets?

Super Auto Pets is a chill auto battler game that you can play on PC or mobile. In the game, you collect and evolve a team of pets, and use them to battle against other players' pets.

How do the weekly tournaments work?

We will place a weekly tournament every Sunday or Monday evening. We will play one game with 10 lives using the weekly pet pack. There are no team restrictions, so feel free to bring your best pets and strategies to the game!

What are the rules and restrictions of the weekly tournaments?

The rules for the weekly tournaments are simple: play one game with 10 lives using the weekly pet pack. There are no team restrictions, so you can bring any pets you want. Registration can happen any time up to the event, and game lobbies will be created 30 minutes prior to start.

What are the prizes and rewards for participating in the weekly tournaments?

The winner of the weekly tournament will receive 1 Corn Kernel, our on-chain currency. After winning 5 times, the winner will receive a mainnet ethereum VIP NFT badge.

Are there any tips or strategies that players should know before participating in the tournaments?

One tip we can offer is that the horse is not as strong as you might think. Be sure to experiment with different pets and strategies to find what works best for you.

When is the next tournament?

Stay tuned for announcements about upcoming tournaments and registration deadlines. We hope to see you at the next Super Auto Pets Weekly Tournament!
